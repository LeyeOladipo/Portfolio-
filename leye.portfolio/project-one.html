FOODIE!: A food delivery app

Role: User Experience Designer, Interface designer

Project Background

A larger population of students and young adults hardly have the time to cook meals of late. Most thrive in obtaining food from restaurants all around them.

The Goal

Design a mobile application called Foodie! This encourages users to get the food of their choosing from any restaurant around them in the comfort of their homes, workplace or classroom.

The Problem Statement

Tioluwanimi is a Dramatic Art student who’s longing to satisfy her craving wherever she can get it. From past experience, she’d usually be too busy with classes and ends up going to bed with either an empty stomach or a bowl of cereal.

The Solution

Foodie! Is a regional online restaurant that helps connect foodies to whatever restaurant around them. With the help of a mobile app and fast connection with dispatch riders within Nigeria, now we can get your meal to you faster and safer.

My Role

User Experience Designer and User Interface Designer.

Project Duration

March 2022 to April 2022



The User

Foodie! is aimed to provide convenience for busy people, who may lack the time or desire to cook, and for whom grocery shopping can be a hassle. These apps allow users to order food from a wide variety of restaurants with just a few taps on their smartphones, also providing them with real-time updates on the status of their orders and estimated delivery times. Additionally, Foodie! also allows users to filter for different dietary needs so they can find meals that are suitable for them.



User Persona

Meet "Foodie!", the ultimate food delivery app for the discerning palate. Whether you're a foodie, gourmet, or just love good food, Foodie! has something for everyone. With a wide range of restaurants, cuisines, and meal options, you'll never get bored. Discover new flavors and food trends, order from your favorite local restaurants, and get food delivered straight to your door in minutes. Upgrade your food game with Foodie!



Wireframe

"Foodie! is a user-friendly food delivery app that makes ordering food fast and simple. The app includes a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate interface, complete with a search bar, filter options, and a variety of categories. Users can browse menus, place orders, track deliveries and make payments all within the app. The app also has a "favorite restaurant" feature, so users can easily reorder their go-to meals. With Foodie! you can have your delicious meals at the doorsteps in minutes.



Design

Foodie! is designed with simplicity and ease-of-use in mind. The app's aesthetic is modern, clean and visually appealing, with a color scheme of appetizing shades of red and orange. Navigation is intuitive and straightforward, with clear iconography and typography. The app uses images of food and restaurant logos to make it easy to identify and select your favorite meals. Overall, Foodie! combines functionality and aesthetic to enhance the user's food delivery experience
